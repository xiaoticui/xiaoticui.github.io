About

I am a researcher at Aalborg University working on hydrogen and e-fuels production. My background is in chemical engineering, with more than 12 years of experience in chemical and electrochemical processes, including modelling and experimental research from reactor scale to system-level applications.

Through close collaboration with industrial partners, I have contributed as a principal researcher and project manager in several research projects funded by Danish and EU programmes. I welcome collaborations with academic and industrial partners in electrolysis, hydrogen technologies, and Power-to-X systems.

Research keywords: water electrolysis, solid oxide electrolyzer, hydrogen technologies, Power-to-X systems, electrochemical reactors, diagnostics

Research Interests

Solid Oxide Electrolysis (SOE) Systems
Dynamic operation and electrochemical control of solid oxide electrolysis cells, stacks, and systems. Development and operation of pressurized solid oxide electrolysis systems.
Diagnostics and Degradation of Electrolysis systems
Development of diagnostic approaches for monitoring degradation phenomena and operational behaviour in water electrolyzer systems.
Flexible Power-to-X Systems and Integration
Dynamic operation and flexibility study on e-fuel plants and Power-to-X systems in future energy system.
AI for energy research
AI-accelerated methodologies for electrolysis research and experimentation.
